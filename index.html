Panhala

Panhala Fort provides a glimpse into the rich history of Maharashtra and offers a unique blend of architectural marvels, natural beauty, and cultural heritage.

Pratapgadh

Pratapgad provides a chance to explore the historical legacy of Shivaji Maharaj, witness splendid architecture, enjoy panoramic views, and immerse oneself in the natural beauty of the region.

Sindhudurg

Sindhudurg Fort provides an opportunity to delve into the rich history of the Maratha empire, witness remarkable architecture, and experience the natural beauty of the Arabian Sea. The fort's strategic location and its underwater treasures make it a popular destination for history buffs, nature lovers, and adventure seekers alike.
